Introduction to CyberAware

Welcome to the CyberAware documentation! CyberAware is your comprehensive suite of tools designed to enhance your online safety and protect your digital identity. Our mission is to empower you with the knowledge and utilities to navigate the digital world securely.

This documentation will guide you through setting up your account, understanding our core features like the Phishing Detector and Password Strength Checker, and how to make the most of CyberAware to safeguard your online presence.

Why CyberAware?

  • Proactive Protection: Identify potential threats before they impact you.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-use tools for everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
  • Educational Resources: Learn about the latest threats and best practices in cybersecurity.
  • Community Focused: Join a community dedicated to online safety.

Account Setup

Setting up your CyberAware account is quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Registration: Navigate to the Sign Up page. Fill in your details, including a strong password.
  2. Email Verification: Check your email for a verification link. Click it to activate your account.
  3. Login: Once verified, head to the Login page and enter your credentials.
  4. Profile Completion (Optional): Visit your Account Details page to add more information or customize your settings.

Managing Your Profile

From your profile, you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage your notification preferences.

Quickstart Guide

Ready to dive in? Here’s how to quickly start using CyberAware's key features:

1. Check a Suspicious Link:

Go to the Phishing Detector, paste the URL, and click "Scan." Review the analysis for potential risks.

2. Evaluate Password Strength:

Use the Password Strength Checker (found under Layanan) to see how robust your passwords are and get tips for improvement.

3. Explore Security Articles:

Browse our collection of articles on various cybersecurity topics to stay informed. These can be found in the "Security Articles" section.

Using the Phishing Detector

Our Phishing Detector helps you identify potentially malicious links before you click on them.

To use the Phishing Detector:

  1. Navigate to the Phishing Detector tool.
  2. Copy the suspicious URL (web address) you want to check.
  3. Paste the URL into the input field provided.
  4. Click the "Scan URL" or "Check Link" button.
  5. Review the detailed analysis, which may include domain information, SSL certificate status, and checks against known phishing databases.
Example: Enter "http://example-suspicious-site.com"

Always exercise caution even if a link is deemed safe. If something feels off, it's better to avoid it.
